Most Popular Course

PADI Open Water Diver

⏱ 3–4 days 🌊 Max depth: 18m 🏅 Worldwide certification

The PADI Open Water Diver course is the world's most popular scuba certification. Complete your course in the warm, clear waters of Camiguin and earn a certification that lets you dive anywhere in the world down to 18 meters.

  • PADI eLearning materials (study at home before arrival)
  • Confined water skills sessions
  • 4 open water training dives
  • All diving equipment included
  • PADI certification card & registration
  • Small group: max 4 students per instructor
₱22,000

per person, includes equipment & certification

Level Up

PADI Advanced Open Water

⏱ 2–3 days 🌊 Max depth: 30m 🏅 Advanced certification

Ready to go deeper? The Advanced Open Water course expands your diving skills with 5 adventure dives including Deep and Underwater Navigation. Perfect preparation for diving Burias Shoal and other advanced sites around Camiguin.

  • 5 adventure dives (Deep, Navigation + 3 electives)
  • Electives: Night Dive, Peak Buoyancy, Fish ID, Drift Dive
  • All diving equipment included
  • PADI certification card & registration
  • Dive at Camiguin's best sites
